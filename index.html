About Ruboni Tourism Village

Ruboni Tourism Village, nestled in the heart of a vibrant forest, offers a unique blend of cultural immersion and eco-tourism. Located in the Rwenzori Mountains of Uganda, this community-driven initiative aims to showcase the rich traditions of the Bakonzo people while preserving the natural environment.

Why Visit Ruboni?

  • Experience traditional Bakonzo hospitality and learn about their customs and rituals.
  • Explore the diverse flora and fauna of the Rwenzori Mountains National Park.
  • Engage in eco-friendly activities such as guided nature walks, birdwatching, and cultural performances.
  • Support sustainable tourism practices that benefit the local community.
  • Shop for authentic crafts and souvenirs handmade by local artisans.

Join us in preserving nature and empowering communities through responsible tourism!

Activities at Ruboni Tourism Village

  • Guided Nature Walks
  • Cultural Performances
  • Traditional Cooking Classes
  • Artisan Workshops
  • Community Storytelling Sessions
